How It Works

📱

1. Browse Pets

Swipe through photos of dogs and cats available for adoption in your area. Each pet has a detailed profile with personality traits.

💝

2. Show Interest

Swipe right on pets you connect with. We'll notify the shelter or adoption agency with your information and introduction.

🏠

3. Meet & Adopt

If the shelter thinks you're a good match, they'll contact you to schedule a meet-and-greet. Start your adoption journey!

Features

🔥

Smart Swiping

Tinder-style interface makes browsing pets fun and intuitive. Swipe right to show interest, left to see the next adorable companion.

🎯

Matching Quiz

Answer a few questions about your lifestyle and we'll match you with pets that fit your activity level, living space, and experience.

🤖

Pet AI Assistant

Got questions about pet care? Our AI assistant provides instant answers about training, nutrition, health, and behavior.

💬

Community Q&A

Read real questions from future pet owners answered by experts. Learn from others' experiences before you adopt.
