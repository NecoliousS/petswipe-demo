"I just adopted a 3-month-old kitten. How often should I feed her? The shelter said 3 times a day but she always seems hungry!"
✓ Veterinarian
Dr. Martinez: 3 times daily is perfect for a kitten! They're growing fast and need the calories. If she seems hungry, you can offer a bit more at each meal, but don't free-feed. Stick to scheduled meals to prevent obesity later.

"My rescue dog is terrified of car rides. He shakes and drools every time. Any tips for helping him overcome this?"
✓ Dog Trainer
Jessica K.: Start slow! Don't even turn the car on yet. Just sit in the car with treats for 5 minutes daily. Next step: turn car on but don't move. Then short 1-minute drives. Build up gradually!
